Cloudiness




The percentage of cloudiness for 771 randomly selected cities appears well-dispersed regardless of the cities distance from the equator (0 degree latitude). However, it appears that a greater number of cities have cloudiness with ~0% or ~90% on 6/23,2018. Furthermore, cities with ~0% cloudiness is greater than ~90% cloudiness.

Also, two of the randomly selected cities, the outliers of the data, has 100% cloudiness. Even though it appears that there is a linear cap a ~90% cloudiness, the outliers can not be ignored. The outliers may indicate equipment limitation, sensor data collection error, or the mere fact that ~100% cloudiness is rare. It is of great interest to collect more samples of api calls of weather data to observe the number of cities that have ~100% cloudiness.
